The Economic Freedom (2019)

An index of economic freedom measures jurisdictions against each other in terms of parameters such as trade freedom, tax burden, judicial effectiveness, and more.

The factors: Property Rights, Judical Effectiveness, Government Integrity, Tax Burden, Government Spending, Fiscal Health, Business Freedom, Labor Freedom, Monetary Freedom, Trade Freedom, Investment Freedom, Financial Freedom

These factors may be weighted according to their influence on economic freedom and compiled into a single score that allows for a ranking. The ranking can be done on a country basis or can look at wider regions or smaller subnational units like states.
